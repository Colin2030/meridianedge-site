Outdated, siloed tech stacks lead to high costs, slow processing, and compliance risks.

Real-World Case Examples

National Crime Agency Transformation Programme (2019-2021)

Source: NCA Annual Report 2020-21, p.37

Challenge: 14 disparate forensic tools with no interoperability, causing evidence transfer failures and version control issues.

What Went Wrong: Piecemeal technology procurement without architectural planning led to siloed systems and 40% analyst time spent on admin tasks.

Our Solution Approach: We design:

  • Centralised forensic data lakes
  • Standardised API frameworks
  • Unified analyst workstations
  • Automated evidence tracking

Result: 92% reduction in evidence transfer errors and 22% increase in examiner productivity with full audit compliance.

Magic Circle Law Firm eDiscovery Overhaul (2022)

Source: Legal Technology Journal Case Study, May 2022

Challenge: £1.8m annual technology spend with 72-hour processing delays at critical litigation phases.

What Went Wrong: Legacy on-premise system couldn't scale for modern data volumes, causing repeated project delays.

Our Solution Approach: We implement:

  • Hybrid cloud architectures
  • Predictive coding systems
  • Real-time collaboration tools
  • Automated processing pipelines

Result: Reduced processing time by 85% and cut technology costs by 60% while improving review accuracy and team collaboration.
